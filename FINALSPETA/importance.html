IMPORTANCE of MEIOSIS

Foundation of All Life

Why Meiosis Matters:

  • Genetic Diversity: Crossing over and independent assortment create variation
  • Reproduction: Enables formation of egg and sperm cells
  • Evolution: Variation is key for natural selection
  • Species Survival: Prevents doubling of chromosome number with each generation

Real-Life Relevance:

  • Explains inherited traits
  • Helps understand genetic disorders
  • Used in breeding and agriculture
